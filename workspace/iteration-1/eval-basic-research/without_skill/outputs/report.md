# Beauty App Ideas Research Report (Baseline — No Skill)

**Prompt**: "I want to explore niche beauty app ideas for 2025. I'm a solo developer..."
**Method**: WebSearch (no structured scripts used)

---

## Key Pain Points Found (Reddit/Beauty Communities)

**1. Information Overload & Conflicting Advice**
- Reddit saw 76% YoY growth in beauty views in 2024; "precision beauty" was the #1 trend
- r/hoodedeyes grew 376%, r/noses 338% — users want hyper-specific solutions
- 64% of skincare users prefer fewer, more effective products but have no trusted tool

**2. Ingredient Complexity & Safety Confusion**
- No mobile-native app checks a whole routine for ingredient conflicts
- Greenwashing complaints up 34% since 2022
- INCIDecoder/SkinSort exist but web-only, per-product (not routine-level)

**3. Product Evaluation Is Fragmented**
- MakeupAlley shut down, leaving a gap for trusted long-term reviews
- Reviews scattered across Reddit, TikTok, Sephora with no demographic filtering

**4. Routine Tracking Lacks Causal Insight**
- "Did this product actually work?" is a top Reddit question
- Apps track photos but can't tell users *what* caused a change

**5. Perimenopause & Hormonal Skin Is Underserved**
- 1B+ women will experience menopause; 85% experience skin changes
- Zero dedicated skincare apps for perimenopausal skin exist

**6. Shade Matching Fails for Deeper Skin Tones**
- Most shade finders undertrained on darker skin tones
- Cross-brand shade equivalency is essentially unsolved
